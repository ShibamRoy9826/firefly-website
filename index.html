How do I get the stuff?

Step 1: Design a complete drone

This includes fuselage, flight controller design, custom control surfaces etc, maybe some accessories etc, control plans (custom firmware, if using that)*, Note that its not a quadcopter but a fixed wing drone!

Step 2: Make a pull request

Once you've designed your drone, make a pull request to this repository

Step 3: Make your drone!

If your PR has been accepted, we ship you the parts, or provide a grant to buy them if they are not in the approved parts list. Make it and share your journey in the slack channel!

FAQ

1) What kind of drone do we make?

A fixed wing drone! unfortunately a quadcopter is more expensive to fulfill

2) I have never designed a PCB/worked with hardware, can I join?

Ofcourse you can! start with the guide or if you face issues, ask in the slack channel!

3) When does it end?

You need to submit a pull request before x date! Submissions made after that will not be accepted :(

4) For how much time do I need to work?

For being eligible for the free parts/grant, you need to spend atleast 20hrs on your drone! Its always great if you can spend even more:)
